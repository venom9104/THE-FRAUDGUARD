Tips for Recognizing Phishing Emails:

  • Be cautious of emails that create a sense of urgency.
  • Check the sender's email address carefully.
  • Avoid clicking on links or downloading attachments from unknown sources.
  • Look for spelling and grammar errors in the email.
  • Verify the authenticity of requests by contacting the organization directly.

Best Practices:

  • Use two-factor authentication where available.
  • Keep your software and antivirus updated.
  • Educate yourself about common phishing techniques.
  • Report suspicious emails to your IT department or email provider.
